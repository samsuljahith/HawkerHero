## WHAT IS IT?

HawkerHero is an AI marketing platform built specifically for small business owners — hawkers, restaurant owners, barbers, retail shops, florists, and service providers — who don't have marketing experience, big budgets, or dedicated teams.

**You upload your menu or product list. The AI does the rest.**

- Analyzes your products against live market trends
- Tells you which products to promote right now
- Creates professional captions, posters, and videos
- Saves everything permanently in campaign history

## HOW IT WORKS (The Flow)

```
Step 1: Create Business Profile (2 minutes)
        → Name, type, location, products/menu
        → Upload CSV or Excel file (instant!)

Step 2: AI Market Analysis (automatic)
        → Reads all your products
        → Searches live market trends
        → Identifies trending items
        → Recommends what to promote

Step 3: Create Campaign
        → Select product to promote
        → AI generates:
           ✍️ Multilingual captions (EN, 中文, BM, தமிழ்)
           🎨 Professional marketing poster
           🎬 10-second or 15-second promo video
           #️⃣ Trending hashtags
           📅 Best posting time

Step 4: Download & Post
        → Download poster as PNG
        → Download video as MP4
        → Copy captions to clipboard
        → Post to Instagram, TikTok, Facebook
```

## WHAT MAKES IT INNOVATIVE?

### 1. Product-Aware AI (Not Generic)
The AI doesn't generate random content. It reads your actual menu/catalog, compares each item against live market data, and tells you WHICH specific product to promote and WHY.

### 2. Live Market Intelligence (Exa Web Search)
Every recommendation is grounded in real-time web data — trending foods, viral content styles, competitor activity, seasonal demand. Not stale templates.

### 3. 6-Agent AI Pipeline
Six specialized AI agents work together in parallel:
- 🧠 Brand Strategist (market research)
- 🖋️ Prompt Engineer (optimizes image/video prompts)
- ✍️ Copywriter (multilingual captions with proven frameworks)
- 🎨 Art Director (professional poster generation)
- 🎬 Video Producer (short-form video creation)
- 🕵️ Quality Reviewer (fact-checks claims)

### 4. Campaign Memory (Mem0)
The platform remembers everything. Every analysis, every campaign, every generated asset is permanently saved. When users return, the AI references what worked before.

### 5. CSV/Excel Upload → Instant Understanding
A restaurant owner uploads their menu Excel file, and within seconds the AI understands their entire business and can make intelligent recommendations.

### 6. Multilingual Content
Captions generated in 4 languages simultaneously: English, Chinese, Malay, Tamil — serving Singapore's diverse market.

### 7. No Technical Knowledge Required
Designed for a 60-year-old hawker uncle who has never used a marketing tool. Step-by-step, plain language, no jargon.
