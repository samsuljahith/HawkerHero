## BACKUP: Q&A ANSWERS

**Q: How is this different from Canva?**
A: Canva gives you templates. You still need to know what to make and what to write. HawkerHero tells you what to promote based on live market data, then creates everything — including video.

**Q: How is this different from just using ChatGPT?**
A: Three differences. First, we're product-aware — we read your actual menu. Second, we have persistent campaign memory. Third, we generate images and videos, not just text. And we do it all in one workflow designed for non-technical users.

**Q: What if the Agnes video API is slow?**
A: Captions and posters generate in under 15 seconds. Video renders asynchronously in 1-3 minutes. The user gets captions and poster immediately — video arrives later. The UI never blocks.

**Q: What about the multilingual captions — are they accurate?**
A: Agnes 2.0 Flash handles Chinese, Malay, and Tamil generation natively. We've tested with native speakers and the quality is strong for social media captions.

**Q: How do you handle businesses with no online presence?**
A: That's exactly who we built this for. We don't depend on website data. We use uploaded menus, catalogs, and profile info — plus live market research about the category and location.

**Q: What's your go-to-market?**
A: Start with Singapore hawkers — there's a tight community. Partner with hawker associations and NEA. Then expand to all SMEs, then Southeast Asia. The product works for any small business type.

**Q: Why not just let the user choose what to promote?**
A: They can. But most don't know what's trending. Our AI recommends first, then the user confirms. It's like having a marketing consultant say "promote this today" — then doing the work for you.
